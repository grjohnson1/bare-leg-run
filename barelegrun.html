5K Walk, Run & Roll at the Federal Center

This event will promote and raise funds for the 2010 National Veterans Wheelchair Games. Over 600 wheelchair athletes from all over the nation, Puerto Rico and Great Britain are expected to come to Denver to compete in various events such as track, field, hand cycling, air guns and much more.

Entry & Registration Info

Entry Fees:

  • $25 per Adults
  • $15 per Child under 12
  • $35 after Sept. 19, 2009

Directions

Federal Center located at 6th and Kipling.

The facility is at West Sixth Avenue and Kipling Street. Go south on Kipling 1/2 mile to Gate 1 of the Denver Federal Center. A photo ID (driver's license) is required to enter.
